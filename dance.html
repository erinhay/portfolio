Em Sachs Dance Benefit 2020


In January of 2020, I had the honor of working with Hélène Chesnais, Éloïse L'Her, and Hannah Lottenberg on the 24th Annual Emily Sachs Dance Benefit. The show brought together the 12 Dance Arts Council groups at the University of Pennsylvania to raise money for the Community Asthma Prevention Program Plus (CAPP+) at CHOP in memory of Emily Sachs, a Penn student who passed away from asthma complications in 1995. While at Penn, Emily was a member of Arts House Dance Company and a vibrant member of the dance community.


I served as Artistic Chair, so my responsibilites were to create marketing and show content for the performance. This content, examples of which you can see below, included posters, transition videos between dances during the show, and social media marketing posts. Together, Hélène, Élo, Hannah, and I raised $7,000 for CHOP CAPP+. Seeing so many people come together for a cause close to the hearts of everyone in DAC was so inspiring to me. I can say without a doubt that working on Em Sachs 2020 was one of my favorite experiences at Penn.

Penn Summer Dance Series 2019

Founded in 2015 by Ramita Ravi (UPenn CAS '18), Penn Summer Dance Series is dedicated to bringing dance to Penn's campus over the summer. Classes are open to anyone, regardless of their level of experience, and taught on a volunteer basis by students and members of the Philadelphia community. In the summers following, the Series lived on thanks to dancers who volunteered to organize the twice-a-week classes.


In 2019, I took over the role of coordinator, after seeking out opportunties to dance on Penn's campus over the summer. In addition to Penn, the 2019 Series attracted teachers from Rutgers and Drexel. By mid-July, Penn Summer Dance Series became so popular, that Fridays were added to the normal schedule of Monday and Wednesday workshops. As a result, we held a total of 24 workshops, the most in any summer to date - almost doubling the number of classes from 2018! With a Facebook group of almost 900 dancers, the Series continues to grow, even amidst the COVID-19 pandemic, with initiatives such as the Penn "Quarantine" Dance Series. There is great hope for our joining together again to celebrate dance in the summer of 2021.

AHTAHs Winter 2018

AHTAHs, or Arts House Teaches Arts House, are a series of workshop-style classes held by Arts House Dance Company at the end of every semester. Company members may opt in to teaching a dance class of whatever style they want. As a freshman, I wanted to dip my toes into the waters of choreography, so I took the oportunity to choreography my first real contemporary combo to Trent Dabbs' "Set For Life." Everyone who took the class was so supportive and made my first glimpse into choreography and teaching dance wonderful!
